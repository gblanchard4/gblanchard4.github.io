About Me

Hello!

I am a New Orleans native that graduated in 2013 from the Univeristy of New Orleans with a degree in Computer Science. My focus was on computer security and information assurance. I have worked as a bioinformatics analysis for Louisiana State University's Health Sciences Center. I worked on new techniques to analyze 16S microbial sequencing for microbiome research. Currently I am working as a DevOps Engineer.

I enjoy hacking on my Raspberry Pis and Arduinos in my free time, it's always fun to see code interacting with the physical world. I love to kayak fish and go AFK every opportunity I can.
